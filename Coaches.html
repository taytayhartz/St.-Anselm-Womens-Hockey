Coaching Staff

The St. Anselm Women's Hockey coaching staff consists of four coaches. One head, two assistants and a goalie coach who work together to help guide the team to victory. Head Coach Sam Ftorek focus's on working the players through their key skills. These inlcude skating, passing, and game strategies. The three coaches support their team both on and off the ice.

Head coach Sam Ftorek leads practices for the team. He also helps design game plays for different situations that the team may face in a game. Ftorek teaches players how to work together and win games when it matters. He also once played college hockey at Saint Anselm.

Assistant Coach Jessica Salisbury also leads practices for the team. She assists in creating strategies with Ftorek. Both soaches also help players as they balance school and athletics. They encourage their athletes to participate in strong academics and good sportsmanship.
